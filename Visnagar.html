Visnagar

Visnagar is a city in the Indian state of Gujarat. It is located in the Mehsana district and is known for its historical significance and cultural heritage. The city has a rich history dating back to ancient times and has been ruled by various dynasties, including the Mauryas, Guptas, and Mughals.

Visnagar is also known for its educational institutions, including the Visnagar College and the Visnagar Institute of Technology. The city has a vibrant culture and is home to several festivals and events throughout the year, including the Navratri festival and the Diwali festival.
